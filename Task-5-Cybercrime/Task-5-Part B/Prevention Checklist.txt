"Stay Safe Online": Prevention Checklist for students :

UPI/Financial safety :
1.Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money or in any other malicious platform.
2.Do not scan random QRs or links sent via unknown sources.

General saftey checklist :
1.Always uses a strong and unique passwords other then your names.
2.Always enable Two-Factor Authentication(2FA) on important accounts.
3.Never click on suspicious links regarding bank or delivery services.
4.Keep your accounts private and avoid over sharing.
5.Always verify any urgent money request before moving further.
6.Always keep update your OS and Apps for latest security patches.
7.Be caution with open or public Wi-Fi.
8.Always logout from shared devices. 

How to report cyber crime :

1.If you are a victim of cyber fraud report an immediate FIR at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
National Cyber Crime portal.
2.Or call on the helpline number for any cybercrime: 1930 (available for-24x7).
