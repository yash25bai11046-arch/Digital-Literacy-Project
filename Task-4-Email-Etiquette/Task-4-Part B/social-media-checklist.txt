Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

Do's:

Do check your privacy settings often.
Do assume everything you post as a permanent digital footprints.
Do use LinkedIn to connect with professionals and professors.
Do fact-check news before sharing it.
Do stay polite in comments and group chats.

Don'ts:

Don't share your exact live location to unknown.
Don't involve in any online propaganda.
Don't engage in online bullying.
Don't post inappropriate photos or content.
Don't log into sensitive accounts on public Wi-Fi.
